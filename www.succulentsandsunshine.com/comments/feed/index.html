]]> Comment on How to Water Succulent Plants by Succulent Success Team https://www.succulentsandsunshine.com/how-to-water-succulent-plants/#comment-286950 Fri, 23 Oct 2020 15:39:05 +0000 http://www.succulentsandsunshine.com/?p=4055#comment-286950 In reply to KJoseph.

Water meters are meant to work with organic soil, and they don’t work well in Bonsai Jack soil. It can take a bit of getting used to, for you as well as your plants, and you might find yourself watering more frequently at first. You definitely don’t want to water every day, though.

To water, we like to plug the bottom of the pot, fill it completely full with water, let it sit for a couple seconds and then let the water run out. If you’re watering every other day, your plants are not establishing a good root system. Start by watering every other day then gradually reduce the watering frequency and the plant will get used to drought.
